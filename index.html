What actually happens on a practice date?

You pick the setting — coffee, drinks, dinner, wherever feels natural. It's a real date, real conversation, no scripts. Afterward you get honest feedback on how you came across — what landed, what didn't, and what to take into every date after this.

Who will I be going on the date with?

Someone who built this from the inside out. Having lived and dated across India, the US, the UK, and Canada, she's seen the same patterns play out across cultures — what women notice, what they never say, and what actually makes someone worth a second date. She's not here to judge you. She's here to give you the one thing nobody else has — an honest perspective, in real time, on a real date. You're in good hands.

Is this confidential?

Completely. What happens on your practice date stays between you and us.

How is this different from a dating coach?

A coach gives you a script. We take it away. The goal here isn't to turn you into someone better at performing — it's to help the real you come through clearly enough that the right person actually sees it. No techniques. No lines. Just you, understood better than before.

How is this different from apps, mixers, or matchmakers?

All of those are trying to find you someone. We're doing something none of them can — sitting across from you, experiencing you the way a woman would, and telling you the truth after. The apps, the mixers, the matchmakers can all work. This is what makes them work better.

Do I need dating experience?

No. Whether you haven't dated in years, just got out of a relationship, or are tired of things not clicking — this is built for exactly that.
